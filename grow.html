grow

1st Place Winner at HackLassonde 2019

Project Time Frame: 24 hours

A look at our world today.

One and a half acres of rainforest is cut every second.

There are 5.25 trillion pieces of plastic debris in the ocean.

Humanity has wiped out 60% of mammals, birds, fish and reptiles since 1970.

Problem Statement

How might we use technology to reduce our Eco footprint in the depletion of oceans, wildlife, and forests and make our world a more sustainable and livable place for future generations?

Questions we asked ourselves.

How can we bring more awareness to these pressing issues on the environment?

How can we show users the carbon footprint of products they buy?

What incentive would users have to continue using the app?

How can we give sustainable start-ups the spotlight they deserve?

How can we incorporate AR and machine learning technology?

Meet Grow.

Grow is an app that harnesses the technology of AR and machine learning to help the user make informative decisions about current and future purchases.

Making the world a greener place one scan at a time.

Features

What incentive would users have to continue using the app?

Set an eco goal. Choose between saving the ocean, forests, or wildlife.

How can we show users the carbon footprint of products they buy?

Scan the company logos on your purchases. Grow uses AR to display the companies' eco rating.

How can we give sustainable start-ups the spotlight they deserve?

Endorse sustainable start-up companies. Products will populate based on the items you scan.

Watch your profile flourish.

The more items you scan and companies you endorse, the more environmentally aware you'll become and the closer you’ll be to reaching your eco goal.
