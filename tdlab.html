Working at the Lab

This year, I was given the amazing opportunity to work as a UI/UX design intern at TDLab, TD Bank's innovation center in Waterloo, Ontario. As my first exposure to the design role, I’ve learned many valuable lessons that will stay with me throughout my design career. Unfortunately, I’m unable to share the corporate work I did for the Lab due to non-disclosure agreements. In the meantime, check out some of the Hackathon projects I worked on during my time at TD!

MyMarket

MyMarket is a mobile app that aims to reduce the barrier of entry to investing by allowing students to invest in companies that align with their values. As my first ever design sprint, this project allowed me to become more familiar with Sketch and do some research on the biggest friction points students face today with finance.

PayPool

For our Mid-Term Hack, my group and I were presented with the problem space for splitting. We asked ourselves, "How can we make splitting money between friends an easier and frictionless experience for students?".

Using the Interac e-transfer API, we created PayPool.

PayPool helps students to conveniently split their expenses by allowing them to create shared pools for each large transaction. In doing so, students can skip the tedious and burdensome process that often comes with splitting money. Instead, they can pool their money with PayPool so that they can pool their time and energy into enjoying that trip they've always wanted to go on.
